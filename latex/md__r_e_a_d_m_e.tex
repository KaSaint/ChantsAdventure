This project implements a text-\/based adventure game in C++. Players navigate a 20-\/node map, collect items, and aim to reach the Treasure Room with the Golden Key to win. The game integrates a simple inventory system, item inspection, and path exploration to create an engaging gameplay experience. \hypertarget{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md3}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{Features}{Features}}\label{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md3}
